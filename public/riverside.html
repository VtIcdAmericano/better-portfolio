Riverside Company

Role Data Engineer Lead & Quantitative Analyst
Date Sep 2025 – Dec 2025
Location Ithaca, New York, U.S.
Type Industry Collaboration · Private Equity

Overview

Worked as a Data Engineer Lead and Quantitative Analyst on a private equity industry project in collaboration with The Riverside Company through Cornell University. The project focused on investment performance analysis and data pipeline development, analyzing over 200 real investment datasets to surface actionable insights around IRR, operating targets, and portfolio-level trends.

Key Contributions

  • Built a Python data pipeline to ingest, clean, and structure investment data for analysis
  • Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA) on 200+ investments across multiple sectors
  • Conducted trend and momentum decay analysis on portfolio performance over time
  • Identified early underperformance signals that correlated with negative IRR outcomes
  • Built a Tableau dashboard to visualize IRR trends, portfolio tracking, and key metrics
